Grains 101

Before we delve into this exciting new world of grains, it is essential to first become acquainted with the basics. First, we’ll examine grain anatomy and what makes up each individual kernel. Then, we’ll follow the journey from field to flour by exploring how grains are grown, harvested, and stored, and subsequently their transformation into flour. Finally, I’ll share insight gleaned from professional bakers so you can successfully bake with and enjoy these local flours.
